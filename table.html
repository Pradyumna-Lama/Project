ISSUES OF IMPORTANCE TO PESIONERS
1. Non Employment Certificate :All pensioners should furnish a certificate of non-employment/re-employment once in a year in prescribed format.
2. Non remarriage Certficate : Widow of decreased pensioner/employee will submit a certificate of non-marriage before commencement of pension with an undertaking to report her remarriage promptly to PDA.
3. Life Certificate : Pensioner/Family pensioner drawing pension through SAMPANN shal have to submit Digital Life Certificate(DLC)/Life Cretificate(LC) once in 6 months form last date of verification.
4. Income Tax : Pensioners may submit details of their income tax savings and other information either online or by sending physical forms.
